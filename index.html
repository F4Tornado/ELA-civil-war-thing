Civil war info

The Civil War revolutionized weaponry and war strategy.

Many new weapons were created during the civil war, for example:

  • Artillery

  • Minefields

  • Rudimentary flamethrowers



The new weapons neccesitated new startegies, and old ones disappearing.



The Repeating Rifle and Carbine made frontal assaults on well defended positions obsolete


The strategy of breaking the enemies will to keep going was also created.

This strategy was used in Sherman's march through Georgia, where enormous quantities of infrastructure were destroyed.
